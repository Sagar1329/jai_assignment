Wholesale Supplies – Spices

Our wholesale division forms the backbone of our business, offering premium quality spices and pulses in bulk 30 kg packaging tailored for B2B clients across India.

Products

We specialize in essential commodities including Cumin, Coriander, Sesame, Mustard, Fenugreek, Fennel, and various Grams - products that drive the majority of our revenue and have earned us a trusted reputation in the market.

Process
  • Procurement: We source directly from farmers and APMC markets, ensuring competitive prices for producers while maintaining quality control from the origin.
  • Fumigation: All raw materials undergo professional fumigation to eliminate pests and ensure safety standards.
  • Cleaning & Sortex: Our Sortex machines scan every single particle, eliminating impurities through advanced optical sorting for consistently premium-grade products.
  • Packaging: Products are packed in durable 30 kg bags, designed for ease of handling and extended shelf life.
