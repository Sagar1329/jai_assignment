Wholesale Supplies – Spices

Bulk Spice Supply for B2B Clients

Our wholesale division forms the backbone of Jamnagar Agro Industries. We supply premium-quality spices in bulk 30 kg packaging to wholesalers, semi-wholesalers, processors, and exporters across India.

Backed by advanced processing infrastructure and consistent sourcing, our products meet strict quality standards and long-term supply commitments.

Our Process

From Farm to Bulk Packaging

  • Procurement: Direct sourcing from farmers and APMC markets
  • Fumigation: Professional pest control to ensure safety standards
  • Cleaning & Sortex: Advanced optical sorting for impurity removal
  • Packaging: Durable 30 kg bags designed for handling and shelf life
