Our Story

Built on Trust Since 2014

Founded in 2014 as Marshall Food Industries, Jamnagar Agro Industries began as a partnership firm focused on processing and trading agricultural commodities. Over the years, the company expanded its footprint across South, West, and Central India, building long-term relationships with wholesalers, brokers, and exporters.

In 2025, the business transitioned into a Limited Company, marking a new phase of growth with expanded manufacturing capacity and entry into branded retail segments.
