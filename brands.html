Our Brands

Brands Built on Quality & Trust

Jamnagar Agro Industries has developed and partnered with brands that reflect our commitment to quality, authenticity, and long-term value. From spices and incense to traditional mouth fresheners, each brand is backed by our strong sourcing and distribution expertise.

Retail Spices

SKP Raja Rani

Launched in 2011 as our retail spice brand, SKP Raja Rani transitioned to wholesale in 2014 alongside our B2B expansion. In 2025, the brand is returning to retail with renewed focus, comprehensive marketing support, and the trust built over 15 years.

Premium Incense

Parampara

Parampara is our in-house incense brand, developed in collaboration with a production partner experienced since the 1990s. From concept to packaging, the brand is entirely designed by our team to deliver premium agarbatti that reflects our quality standards.

Traditional Mouth Fresheners

Ras Pan

Ras Pan is the result of our exclusive partnership with Jamnagar’s renowned mukhwas makers, Jamnagari Mukhwas, a family business serving since 1980. Together, we combine authentic Jamnagari expertise with our distribution strength to reach wider markets.

Jamnagar Agro Industries is a trusted processor and supplier of agricultural commodities, delivering quality-driven solutions across India.
