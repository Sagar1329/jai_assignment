Our Brands

Brands Built on Quality & Trust

Jamnagar Agro Industries has developed and partnered with brands that reflect our commitment to quality, authenticity, and long-term value. From spices and incense to traditional mouth fresheners, each brand is backed by our strong sourcing and distribution expertise.

Retail Spices

SKP Raja Rani

A brand with heritage and evolution. Launched in 2011 as our retail offering, Raja Rani transitioned to wholesale in 2014, growing alongside our B2B expansion. Now in 2025, we're bringing it back to retail with comprehensive marketing support, combining 15 years of trust with fresh consumer focus.

Premium Incense

Parampara

Our in-house incense brand crafted with a production partner seasoned since the 1990s. From concept to packaging, Parampara is entirely designed by our team, offering premium agarbatti that reflects our commitment to quality across every category we enter.

Traditional Mouth Fresheners

Ras Pan

Our exclusive partnership with Jamnagar's legendary mukhwas makers, Jamnagri mukhavas, a family business serving since 1980, now in its 4th generation. Together, we've created Ras Pan, blending their authentic Jamnagari expertise with our distribution strength to bring traditional mouth fresheners to a wider market
